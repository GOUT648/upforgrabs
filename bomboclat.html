About

THE SECONDARY MARKET, ELEVATED.

Up For Grabs started from a simple observation: the world's most desirable objects were locked behind private dealer networks, opaque auction house markups, and grey-market chaos. We built a cleaner alternative.

Every item in our inventory is sourced directly, authenticated by certified professionals, and priced to be taken seriously — not inflated for theatre. No bidding wars, no reserve-price theatre, no buyer's premium. Just coveted objects at transparent prices.

A class project by Gheorghe Chirica, 10th Form B — built with the ambition and execution of a real operation. All items are fictional for educational purposes.
